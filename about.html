FRANCESCA BRIERLEY is a digital artist working in graphic, motion and web design.

She has a BSc in Digital Arts Computing from Goldsmiths University of London, from which she graduated with First Class Honours in 2021.
While attending her course, she organised and participated in several computational art exhibitions and worked as web and graphic designer for the audiovisual journal Sonic Scope.

Exploring art through technology was the focus of her studies and informes her output across different styles and media.

She is an active part of the London music scene and her most recent work has been focused around creating assets and promo material for musicians, labels and live music events, alongside her personal projects.
